Why Art Transcends Medium: A Conversation with Sculptor Rehan Iqbal

Author Leslie
Published Aug 22, 2025

In a world where art meets emotion, sculptor Rehan Iqbal explains how form and light converge to evoke stories beyond the frame. His work breathes life into static materials, forging connections that span time and space.

Reflections on Material and Meaning

The tactile voice of stone, the silent resonance of metal — Rehan’s sculptures invite viewers to listen with their eyes. “I carve because I believe every material has its own soul,” he shares. Through matte textures and polished surfaces, Rehan explores memory, transformation, and improvisation.

The Gallery Journey: Concept to Exhibit

Conceptualization: Starting with sketches and a deep conversation about emotion and context.

Creation: Material sourcing + hands-on shaping of clay, metal, or plaster under deliberate lighting.

Presentation: Final pieces lit in dark spaces with focused neon-accent highlights—creating immersive experiences that draw in the viewer.
