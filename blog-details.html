Why Art Transcends Medium: A Conversation with Sculptor Rehan Iqbal

Author Leslie
Published Aug 22, 2025

In a world where art meets emotion, sculptor Rehan Iqbal explains how form and light converge to evoke stories beyond the frame. His work breathes life into static materials, forging connections that span time and space.

Reflections on Material and Meaning

The tactile voice of stone, the silent resonance of metal — Rehan’s sculptures invite viewers to listen with their eyes. “I carve because I believe every material has its own soul,” he shares. Through matte textures and polished surfaces, Rehan explores memory, transformation, and improvisation.

The Gallery Journey: Concept to Exhibit

Conceptualization: Starting with sketches and a deep conversation about emotion and context.

Creation: Material sourcing + hands-on shaping of clay, metal, or plaster under deliberate lighting.

Presentation: Final pieces lit in dark spaces with focused neon-accent highlights—creating immersive experiences that draw in the viewer.

Q1 : What is the concept behind this Art Gallery?

Our gallery blends tradition with technology, showcasing everything from classic paintings to digital and AI-generated art. It’s designed to make art more immersive and accessible to all.

Q2 : Do I need to create an account to explore the gallery?

No, you can freely explore our gallery and view exhibitions without an account. However, creating one allows you to save favorites, book consultations, and access exclusive content.

Q3: Are the artworks available for purchase?

Yes, selected artworks showcased in our exhibitions are available for purchase. Each piece includes details on availability, pricing, and purchase inquiries.

Q4: Can I attend exhibitions virtually?

Absolutely! We provide immersive virtual tours and digital showcases, enabling you to experience exhibitions from anywhere in the world.

Q5: How often are new exhibitions added?

We update our exhibitions quarterly, featuring fresh themes, new artists, and exclusive collections every season.
